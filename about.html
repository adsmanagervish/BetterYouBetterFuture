👋 Hey, I'm Vish

Welcome to Better You Better Future — a space I created because I believe that small changes in how we think and behave can create big changes in society.

I'm a young adult from India, just like many of you. I grew up watching society put people in boxes — telling them how to behave, what to feel, and who to be. And I got tired of it.

This blog is my way of starting a conversation. About mental health. About patriarchy and gender norms. About the small everyday things we can all do differently to build a better world — starting with ourselves.

I'm not an expert. I'm just someone who thinks deeply, feels strongly, and believes that you deserve to be heard and understood.

So if you've ever felt like society's rules don't make sense, or that you're carrying too much alone — you're in the right place. 💛

— Vish
